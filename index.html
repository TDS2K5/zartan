Our Story

Craftsmanship Born From Passion

Founded on the belief that automotive legends deserve a second chapter, Zartan Restomods has spent over two decades perfecting the art of the restomod. We see beyond rust and patina to the soul of each vehicle — then bring it roaring back to life with modern reliability, performance, and safety.

Every project begins with a conversation, not a contract. We listen to your vision, understand your connection to the marque, and craft a restoration plan that honors both the car's heritage and your individual story.

Heritage First

We preserve the character that makes each classic iconic, ensuring authenticity in every curve and contour.

Modern Soul

Beneath period-correct exteriors lie fuel-injected engines, modern suspensions, and contemporary safety systems.

Bespoke Always

No two Zartan builds are alike. Each is tailored to the owner's vision, driving style, and intended use.

Built to Drive

These aren't trailer queens. Our restomods are engineered for miles of smiles, not museum display.
